\# AGENTS.md



\## Project



This project is PANSK, an AI-assisted streetwear brand website prototype.



PANSK is not a generic ecommerce template.

PANSK is not a Nike clone.

PANSK is a streetwear brand showcase with product catalog and lookbook / life archive feeling.



The emotional center of the brand is:



“想到你穿这件衣服去天涯海角，我就很高兴，用最好的样子去见最好的人。”



Do not rewrite this Chinese philosophy copy unless explicitly requested.



\## How to Use DESIGN.md



DESIGN.md may contain a reference design system such as Nike.



Use DESIGN.md only as an art-direction reference.



Borrow:

\- black / white contrast

\- full-bleed photography

\- strong image hierarchy

\- large typography

\- product confidence

\- minimal text

\- restrained buttons

\- premium visual rhythm



Do not copy:

\- Nike branding

\- Nike tone of voice

\- Nike ecommerce structure

\- Nike product navigation

\- Nike sports campaign language

\- large corporate retail feeling



The goal is to make PANSK feel like a high-end streetwear brand website, not a fake Nike website.



\## Current Design Direction



PANSK should feel:

\- editorial

\- streetwear

\- clean white

\- black / white / gray

\- image-led

\- emotional but restrained

\- product catalog + life archive

\- quiet but strong



Avoid:

\- colorful gradients

\- heavy shadows

\- generic ecommerce cards

\- fake interactive controls

\- excessive animation

\- too many font styles

\- too much explanatory copy

\- making every section look like a separate poster



\## Section Roles



Hero:

\- visual impact only

\- full-screen campaign image

\- minimal text

\- no long explanation



Brand Philosophy:

\- emotional heart of the website

\- keep Chinese copy unchanged

\- quiet manifesto

\- strong whitespace

\- should transition naturally into products



Products:

\- clean product wall

\- large product images

\- small catalog-like text

\- avoid generic ecommerce card styling



Lookbook:

\- life archive

\- real outfits entering real life

\- large image + minimal editorial text

\- should not feel like a random carousel

\- first priority is layout and mood, not complex animation



Footer:

\- simple final brand impression

\- no clutter



\## Development Rules



\- Do not modify /shop unless explicitly requested.

\- Do not delete existing images or assets.

\- Do not add large dependencies unless approved.

\- Prefer editing existing JSX/CSS.

\- Do not rewrite the whole project at once.

\- Modify one section at a time when possible.

\- After code changes, run npm run build.

\- Summarize changed files, design decisions, and build result.
